# WSO2 API Management Governance

Governing how artifacts such as APIs and MCP Servers are managed throughout their lifecycle has become a critical priority for many modern organizations. It encompasses a set of processes, practices, roles, and responsibilities aimed at ensuring the effective and efficient management of these artifacts. Establishing a governance strategy is essential for maintaining consistency, security, and compliance in artifact development, management, and consumption.

Such a governance strategy provides the following benefits:

- **Security and Compliance** – Enforces authentication, authorization, and regulatory compliance requirements.
- **Consistency** – Standardizes artifact design, documentation, and versioning to maintain uniformity.
- **Performance and Reliability** – Enables monitoring, rate limiting, and policy enforcement to ensure reliable artifact behavior.
- **Lifecycle Management** – Supports structured artifact development, deployment, deprecation, and retirement processes.
- **Collaboration** – Defines clear roles and responsibilities for artifact stakeholders, improving coordination.
- **Discoverability and Reusability** – Facilitates artifact cataloging and reuse to minimize redundant development efforts.
- **Business Agility** – Accelerates artifact delivery, helping organizations meet customer and partner needs efficiently.

WSO2 API Manager provides a range of governance capabilities, and this section outlines how to use them for effective governance.
